Location: Specifically where device is located and transaction was made; you may update preferences to disable this feature.

Authentication: Details from sources to verify identity, including banks and credit bureaus. We may legally obtain background reports from public records.

Payter may collect the following:

Identity: Account sign-in details, including name, address, phone number, email, birthday, location, social security, taxpayer ID.

Finance: Credit or debit card numbers, bank account, credit reports.

Other: Transaction details, tax information, and data you provide, including survey responses, participation in marketing opportunities, and content uploads.

Payter will not collect any data from children 13 years old or younger.
